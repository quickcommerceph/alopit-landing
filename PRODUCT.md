## Users

Filipino sabong fans and bettors, reached almost entirely on mobile phones —
often on cellular data, checking in between rounds, during commutes, or late at
night when the live rooms are hottest. They are emotionally invested in the
sport, fluent in the local cash-in/cash-out rhythm (GCash, Maya, QRPH), and
intolerant of friction: a stream that buffers or a withdrawal that stalls loses
them.

A secondary audience is prospective **affiliates / agents** — operators looking
to build a downline and earn turnover-based commissions through the Gold /
Silver / Bronze tier system.

**Job to be done:** feel the live action ringside from anywhere, move money in
and out on their own terms, and trust the platform enough to register and stay.

## Product Purpose

Alopit is a premium E-Sabong live-streaming + wagering platform. This site is
its acquisition surface — a single marketing landing page that exists to:

1. **Convert visitors into registered players** at `alopitclub.biz`.
2. **Recruit affiliate agents** into the tiered commission program.
3. **Cross-sell the Alowin sportsbook and casino** as the natural next room.

Success is measured in registrations, agent sign-ups, and cross-platform
activation — not page views. Every section is an argument for the next CTA.

## Brand Personality

**Intense. Premium. Filipino-rooted.**

The dominant feeling is *the pulse* — the adrenaline and electricity of
ringside, condensed into a screen. The voice is confident and direct, never
shouting: short, declarative lines, the cadence of a live call ("LIVE ACTION",
"REAL PASSION", "ORIGINAL LIVE FEED"). It carries the heritage and grit of the
sport while wrapping it in production quality that outclasses the category.
Filipino language cues ("MAS SIMPLE", "MAS CONVENIENT") signal belonging, not
novelty.

Emotional goals: urgency, electricity, trust-through-competence, pride.

## Anti-references

Explicitly do **not** look like:

- **Cheap cluttered online-casino.** Vegas-neon spam, blinking banners, busy
  tile walls, maximal low-end e-sabong/casino visual noise.
- **Generic corporate fintech.** Sterile blue-chip SaaS / banking aesthetic
  that erases the sport's heritage and grit in pursuit of "respectability".
- **Western SaaS / AI template.** Inter-for-everything, purple-to-blue
  gradients, cream cards, the rounded-square icon tile above every heading —
  the default AI-generation look.
- **Cartoonish / mascot gambling.** Animated rooster mascots, cartoon chips,
  childish iconography. The sport is treated with weight, not caricature.

## Design Principles

1. **The pit, not the casino.** Channel the raw electricity of the live sport.
   Never borrow the visual language of low-end online casinos; intensity comes
   from the action and the atmosphere, not from clutter.
2. **Premium by restraint, not by gloss.** The near-black canvas and a
   disciplined gold / red / blue palette do the work. Let contrast, type, and
   motion carry the energy; resist the urge to add more elements.
3. **Make the pulse visible.** Live signals (LIVE NOW states, momentum, the
   marquee) must feel alive — the page should hum with the sense that something
   is happening right now.
4. **Filipino-first, world-class finish.** Local payments, local language, and
   local context are features, not afterthoughts — wrapped in production craft
   that visibly outclasses competing platforms in the category.
5. **Every screen earns the click.** Acquisition is the job. Hierarchy, motion,
   and copy all funnel toward register / deposit / agent-sign-up; nothing is
   decorative for its own sake.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Follow standard accessibility best practice as a baseline: sufficient color
contrast, keyboard reachability, semantic markup, and meaningful text
alternatives. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` (the page already gates Framer
Motion entrances on it). Avoid encoding state by color alone where a label or
icon can disambiguate.
